About us

Mzizi is a Swahili word that means root.

The founding belief is solving the problems from the root. Mzizi Empowerment Initiative is an organization based in Kenya. Our current focus areas are the slums of Nairobi. We believe that empowerment is the solution to all social problems in the society. Once a society is empowered, they are free from the consequences and challenges associated with the same. Our focus population are the Youths, teenagers and Women.

Mission

To be an organization that empowers the community towards a wholesome Psycho-social and economic stability by establishing healthy relationships and partnerships with all stakeholders involved towards the same.


Vision

To have a community that is Psychologically, socially and economically empowered wholesomely.

Our Theory of change

After research on existing intervention programs on social problems, we realised that most of them are curative and thus the problems tend to re-occur since the cause is still existing. Due to this, we focus mainly on identifying the problem, the cause and then working on prevention of this root cause. Before implementation, we do a comprehensive study on the cause of the problem and focus on the root cause. We focus more to preventive mechanisms as compared to curative mechanisms.
